Career and Professional Development Center

Gonzaga University


Welcome to the Gonzaga University Career and Professional Development Center, located at Crosby! Our center is dedicated to helping Gonzaga students and alumni achieve their career goals by providing a wide range of services and resources.

At the heart of our mission is the belief that every student deserves the opportunity to pursue a fulfilling career that aligns with their interests and values. Whether you're a freshman just starting to explore your options, a senior getting ready to enter the job market, or an alumnus looking to make a career change, we are here to support you at every stage of your journey.

Our team of experienced career advisors is committed to helping you clarify your goals, develop a plan, and take action to achieve your desired outcomes. We offer individual career coaching sessions, resume and cover letter reviews, mock interviews, job search strategies, networking events, career fairs, and more.

In addition, we collaborate with employers, alumni, and community partners to provide a variety of career-related opportunities for Gonzaga students and alumni. Our goal is to connect you with the resources and people you need to succeed in your chosen field.

At the Gonzaga University Career and Professional Development Center, we believe that career development is an ongoing process that extends beyond graduation. We are committed to providing lifelong support to our alumni as they navigate their careers and pursue their professional goals.

We invite you to explore our website, visit our office, and connect with us on social media to learn more about how we can help you achieve your career aspirations. Thank you for choosing Gonzaga University, and we look forward to supporting you on your journey!
